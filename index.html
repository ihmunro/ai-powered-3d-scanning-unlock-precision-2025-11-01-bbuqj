About Our Mission

Founded in 2020 by a team of AI researchers and precision engineering experts, our company emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: to democratize advanced 3D scanning technology through artificial intelligence. Our founders recognized that while 3D scanning was becoming essential across industries, the process remained time-consuming, error-prone, and expensive. They assembled a diverse team of machine learning engineers, optical specialists, and manufacturing veterans to create a solution that would fundamentally change how the world captures and processes dimensional data. Today, we serve over 500 companies across manufacturing, aerospace, healthcare, and automotive sectors, processing millions of scans annually with unparalleled accuracy and speed.

Our core mission is to empower organizations worldwide by making precision 3D scanning intelligent, accessible, and economical. We believe that by combining cutting-edge artificial intelligence with proven engineering principles, we can eliminate inefficiencies, reduce costs, and unlock new possibilities for innovation. We're committed to continuous improvement, customer success, and advancing the state of the art in scanning technology. Our values of transparency, reliability, and customer-first thinking guide every decision we make, ensuring that our platform not only meets but exceeds the expectations of the most demanding industries.
